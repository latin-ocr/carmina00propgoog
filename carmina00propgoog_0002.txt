EX LIBRIS

4,-

Sumtyme my booke is vnto me A God‚
Sumtyme l throwe it from me A rodd.
All kynde of boolws, good, and badd,
Sayntish and Diuelish‚ that ar to be hadd.

Owlde‚ andyunge,
For matter and tange,
W heresoeuer they dwell‚

In Heauen, or in Hell.

DOUGLAS BUSH
